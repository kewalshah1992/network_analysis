From the above bubble chart, it is clearly seen that the top routes are over used A route here can be assumed as a transortation lane with limited capacity. As most of the sales are also done on these routes, there is only a limited capacity which can be transported each day. This leads to the product being held at the warehouse of the agencies and they are late to reach to the selling shelves which then get expired and are returned Recommeneded Solution - Identify other agencies in the same area which might have other under utilised routes and distribute the load amongst them.To forecast product demand - --> arrange the data to display weekly product wise sales and returns for evety client --> subtract the next weeks returns from the current weeks sales i.e if x units were ordered in week 3 and y units were returned in week 4, subtract week 4 returns from week 3 sales --> Doing this we will get the actual sales for the weeks for all procucts and for every client --> Then we can try differnet forecast models.
